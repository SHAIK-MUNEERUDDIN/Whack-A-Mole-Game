Instructions

Welcome to Whack-a-Mole Game! Your objective is to hit as many moles as you can within the given time limit.

Use your mouse to click on the moles when they appear. Each successful hit will earn you points.

Scoring

Score 10 points for each mole you hit successfully. Be careful not to hit any empty holes, as it will result in a penalty of 5 points.

Difficulty Levels

Choose from three difficulty levels:

  • Easy: Slower moles, suitable for beginners.
  • Medium: Faster moles, providing a moderate challenge.
  • Hard: Very fast moles, for expert players looking for a tough challenge.
